Tools: Merging Duplicates

This tool removes duplicates (repetitions) among persons in the database. The tool shows a prompting window where you must manually select a record to remain and which record to delete (or give this choice to GEDKeeper). When the removed person record has links, they may be moved into the remaining person.

The merging duplicates tool also processes notes, families, and sources.

It is recommended to search duplicates with the "Check the year of birth" option turned on ("Options" tab). This may reduce the number of false operations.
