Archives

Main electronic library "Russian literature and folklore" (FEB)
Full-text information system that contains products of Russian literature and philology, bibliographies, scientific researches, and historical and biographical works. The main content of the FEB is exposed as electronic scientific editions—each dedicated to an individual author (Pushkin, Lermontov, etc.), or genre (Russian epics, songs, etc.), or literary work ("The Lay of Igor's Host", etc.). The following is the FEB's features list: precise information and its description, systemic approach to information formatting, advanced means for navigation ,and search.
