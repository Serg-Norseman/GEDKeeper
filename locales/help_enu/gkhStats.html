Statistics

A statistics window shows summary information about the people stored in the database.

The window contains a summary at the top and statistics on several dedicated categories at the bottom.

There is drop down list of dedicated categories of statistic in the bottom part. A data list is below and a diagram for some categories at the bottom right corner.

When a filter is applied to person records, the statistics are shown for the filtered result only.

The result of each category of statistics may be exported to an Excel workbook (using the button).
