Statistics

The statistics window is found in the "Pedigree" menu or by the [Ctrl + T] hotkey in the main window. It shows summary information about people stored in the database.

The window has three parts: summary at the top, statistics on several dedicated categories at the bottom

There is drop down list of dedicated categories of statistic in the bottom part. The data list is below and a diagram for some categories is at the bottom right.

When a filter is applied to person records, the statistics shown is for the filtered results only.

Result of each category statistics may be exported to Excel (via the button).
