Group Record

Groups make navigation easy—they group people by a common social identifier.

For example, you can create the "Participants in The Great Patriotic War" group with respective members. Or create a "Correspondents" group for people you are currently staying in contact with.

Adding a new person to a group may be done by any of the following methods:

  • Click the "Add" button () on the "Members" tab of the group editing dialog.
  • Click the "Add" button () on the "Groups" tab of the group editing dialog.

Using the "Remove" button () from the same tab, you can remove persons from a group.

There is a table on the "Groups" tab of the main working window where you can find all groups stored in the database. Each group record has a summary area to the right of the table, where you can view entire information about a group and a full list of members.
