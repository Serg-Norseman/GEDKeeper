Access Management

There are three types of access restrictions:

  • Confidentially -- confidential data that never appears in reports, diagrams, or exported content.
  • Locked -- no one can edit locked data without previously unlocking the data.
  • Private -- currently this access restriction is not implemented. A future release will encrypt all private data.

There are three modes of processing protected data:

  •  Maximum protection -- GEDKeeper does not show neither confidential nor private data.
  •  Medium protection -- all data is visible except private data.
  •  No protection -- GEDKeeper shows any data.

Modes of processing protected data affect three data views: tables of person and family records, tree diagrams, and pedigrees. Maximum protection is on by default. You can change the protection mode by double-clicking the mode icon at bottom right corner of the main window.
