A Link to Source

A link to source is a specialized link valid only for person, family, and event records that bind to a concrete information source. A link to source has three additional properties: page or sheet number, where the mentioning was made, and authenticity (certainty) level of the information. Having a page or sheet number is helpful when you need to review the source. An authenticity level quickly shows you how much a source of information may be trusted. When a source document has blots or damage and cannot be read with assurance, we recommend assigning the "Questionable reliability of evidence" level to the source. This can help prevent false conclusions.

Authenticity level also affects the authenticity index which is calculated for a person in a statistics module to analyze mean values. Tree diagrams use the authenticity level to make displayed information more obvious.
