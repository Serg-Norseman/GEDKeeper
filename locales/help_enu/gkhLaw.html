Law

For the Russian Federation

Constitution of the Russian Federation, Clause 1 Article 23

Everyone shall have the right to privacy, personal and family secret, honor and reputation protection.

Constitution of the Russian Federation, Clause 1 Article 24

No one shall collect, store, use and spread privacy information about a person without the person's consent.

The Federal Law of the Russian Federation No 152-FL "personal data", July 27, 2006

For the United States of America

Privacy Act of 1974. The Privacy Act of 1974 (5 U.S.C.A. 552a)

A federal law that places restrictions on the federal government's collection, use, and dissemination of personal information.
(Personal privacy laws are controlled state-by-state.)
