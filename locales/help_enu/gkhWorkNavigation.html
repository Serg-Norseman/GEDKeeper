Navigation

One of the major GEDKeeper targets is to provide the very efficient navigation.

The main window of GEDKeeper allows quick access to six main categories of records with a summary area to the right of the lists. There you can easily view information about a record and use hypertext links to go to another, related record.

The following record types are self-contained:

  • Notes -- any text added to a person, family or event. When a respective option is on, notes can be viewed on a pedigree image.
  • Multimedia content -- any kind of photo, an audio record, or video record that you attached to person, family, or event. You can also add text or other types of documents.
  • Sources -- a special object that stores information about an archive or other types of documents or articles.
  • Groups -- an object that can unite different individuals using some common attribute or relation. For example, you can create "The Great Patriotic War veterans" group and use it to quickly find all persons who participated in WWII.

To access a list of records of each type, you use tabs.

The column lists display the most important information.

You can use any column to order a list by clicking a column header.

The record summary area shows links to other related records as blue underlined text. Clicking such text jumps you to a respective record in this, or another, list.
